    About Us

    We Care For Your Dental Health

    Atuan Dental Clinic is a family-owned company and has been in the dental industry for more than twenty-eight years. The company is an active member of Philippine Dental Association, Manila Dental Chapter Pasig-Pateros-Taguig Dental Chapter and also certfiedby DENR (Department of Environment and Natural Resources) as PCO Pollution Control Officer.

    The founder and sole proprietor, Dr Imelda Atuan is an alumna of Centro Escolar University Manila graduated in the year 1990. Dra. Imelda opened her first dental clinic in their ancestral house located at 1284 Mataas Na Lupa St Malate Manila in 1993. Dra. Imelda Atuan is a General Dentist with a specialization in Orthodonticshusband while Mr. Andres C. Atuan Jr is a registered dental technologist that caters all the dental laboratory works like fabrication of dental prosthesis such as partial or complete dentures, bridges and dental crowns.
